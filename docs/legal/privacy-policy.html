Privacy Policy

LeapfrogIQ Apps is built on a simple principle: your files stay on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your uploaded content.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

LeapfrogIQ Apps ("we," "our," or "us") provides free browser-based utility tools at leapfrogiq.app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Our tools are designed so that all file processing happens locally in your browser. When you upload a file (such as a logo image for conversion), it is processed by JavaScript running on your own device — not sent to any server.

2. Files You Upload

We do not store your uploaded files. Files uploaded to any LeapfrogIQ Apps tool are:

  • Processed locally in your browser using JavaScript.
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third-party server.
  • Not retained in any database or file system.
  • Cleared from memory when you close or refresh the page.

3. Account Data

LeapfrogIQ Apps does not require an account to use any tool. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or email addresses for tool usage.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as page view counts) to understand which tools are most useful. If analytics are used, they:

  • Do not track personal identifiers.
  • Do not track the content of uploaded files.
  • Are aggregated and anonymous.

5. Cookies

LeapfrogIQ Apps does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Any cookies that may be set are strictly functional (e.g. browser session state) and contain no personal data.

6. Third-Party Services

Our tools load fonts from Google Fonts and may load open-source JavaScript libraries (such as Imagetracer.js, JSZip, FileSaver.js). These requests may log your IP address as a standard server log. We do not control third-party data practices — please review their respective privacy policies.

7. Children

LeapfrogIQ Apps tools are general-purpose utilities not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as tools are added or practices change. Updates will be reflected by a new "last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@leapfrogiq.ai or visit the Contact page.

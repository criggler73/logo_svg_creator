Why Extract PDF Pages in the Browser?

Traditional PDF tools require either desktop software like Adobe Acrobat Pro (expensive) or uploading your file to a cloud service. Both options are slow and raise privacy concerns, especially for sensitive documents like contracts, medical records, or financial reports.

This tool uses PDF.js to render page previews and pdf-lib to build the output PDF — entirely within your browser tab. Your file is never transmitted anywhere.

  • Works with any PDF: text, images, forms, mixed content
  • No file size limits enforced by a server — your device's memory is the only constraint
  • Instant results — no waiting for upload or processing queues
  • 100% free, no account required, no watermarks on the output

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract pages from a PDF?

Upload your PDF using the file picker or drag and drop. All pages will render as clickable thumbnails. Click any thumbnail to toggle it between Keep (green border) and Skip (faded grey). You can also type a page range like 1, 3, 5-7. When your selection is ready, click Download to save a new PDF with only those pages.

Is my PDF kept private?

Yes — completely. This tool runs 100% in your browser. Your PDF is read locally on your device, processed in memory, and the output downloads directly to your computer. No data ever leaves your device and nothing is stored on any server.

What page formats are supported?

The tool works with any standard PDF regardless of page size or orientation — A4, Letter, Legal, landscape, portrait, or mixed-size documents. It handles PDFs with text, images, forms, and complex layouts. Very large PDFs may take a moment to render thumbnails but will process correctly.

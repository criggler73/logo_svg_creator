How to Extract Pages from Multiple PDFs at Once

Step 1

Upload Your PDFs

Drop your entire batch of PDF files at once. Each file is loaded locally — nothing is sent to a server.

Step 2

Pick Pages to Keep

Click page thumbnails to mark them Keep or Skip. Or type a range like 1, 3, 5-7. Your pattern is saved across the whole batch.

Step 3

Preview & Confirm

See exactly how many pages will be extracted from each file before committing. Files missing certain pages are flagged automatically.

Step 4

Download Merged PDF

All kept pages from all files are merged into one clean PDF in document order. Plus a CSV report of exactly what was extracted.

Why Use a Batch PDF Page Extractor?

If you regularly work with reports, invoices, contracts, or construction documents where you only need a few pages from dozens of files, extracting them one-by-one in Adobe Acrobat is painfully slow. This tool lets you define your page pattern once and apply it to an entire folder of PDFs in seconds — all without installing software or uploading files to a third-party server.

  • Perfect for batches of reports where different files have different page counts
  • Files missing the requested pages are skipped gracefully — not dropped entirely
  • Save your page pattern as a JSON session file to reuse on next week's batch
  • 100% client-side: your PDFs never leave your device
